Mailvelope

Mailvelope is a browser extension that enables the exchange of encrypted emails following the OpenPGP encryption standard.

No copy and paste required: Mailvelope adds the controls to encrypt and decrypt emails directly into your favorite Webmail provider.

Can be configured to work with basically any Webmail provider.

With Mailvelope the advantages of a cloud based Webmail solution can be combined with OpenPGP encryption.

Generate keys and exchange them with others. Import existing GPG or PGP keys.
